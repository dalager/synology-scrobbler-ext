Where is your Audio Station?

In order to recognize the Audio Station player, you will have to enter the url of your Diskstation


http(s)://
i.e. myserver.dyndns.org
i.e. myserver.gotdns.com:5000

This is the string that the extension will match against the pages you visit to determine if it should try to extract songs from the page you're visiting.

By setting this address to "bungalow" Synology Scrobbler will try to extract songs from bungalow.com, my.bungalow-heaven and cowabungalow.co.uk

Note: AudioStation 5.x and MiniPlayer

As AudioStation 5.0 has changed its interface, the scrobbler will not support scrobbling when using the Mini Player.
The Artist name has been removed from the player, preventing song identification.
